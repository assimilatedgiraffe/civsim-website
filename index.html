Bio

We're an alternative 5-piece band from Belfast. We play music influenced by everything from electronica to funk and from rock to dub reggae.

We've been together as Urban Scarecrow since May 2010 but some of our current members have joined much more recently. We've played a lot around Belfast and elsewhere in NI and even managed a set in England a while back. We decided it was time for a change of directions. The music is changing - and now we're called Civil Simian. The EP is done, and now we're working on post-production for it and we're making our first full length album.
